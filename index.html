01 — About

Who I Am

I'm Avi Aggarwal, a high school student with a deep passion for understanding how political and economic forces shape the world. Whether I'm analyzing fiscal policy, debating electoral systems, or researching inequality, I'm driven by one question: why do systems work the way they do?

I believe that rigorous thinking and clear writing are the most powerful tools for change. My work sits at the intersection of data and narrative — translating complex ideas into arguments that resonate.

I'm actively seeking opportunities in policy research, economics, and public affairs as I prepare for college and beyond.

02 — Focus Areas
⚖

Political Economy

How political institutions shape economic outcomes — and how economic power reshapes politics. Particularly interested in campaign finance, regulatory capture, and democratic backsliding.

📊

Fiscal Policy

Tax systems, public spending, and the tradeoffs governments navigate. I use data to interrogate whether policy actually achieves what it promises.

🌐

Global Affairs

Trade, geopolitics, and international institutions. I'm especially drawn to U.S.–China economic relations and the architecture of global governance.

✍

Public Argument

Writing, debate, and rhetoric as tools for civic life. I believe in making complex ideas accessible without losing their rigor.
